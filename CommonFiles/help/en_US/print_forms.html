EDITING PRINTABLE FORMS

For printing RepairCenter use QtRPT library. To create a template of printable forms use a program QtRptDesigner.


Short reference of QtRptDesigner. Full documentation can be found at qtrpt.sourceforge.net.


Order

Printed when you place a new order.


Filename - ./print_forms/order.xml


Available fields:


Tag:

Field in the screen form:

[number]

Order #

[date]

Date

[customer]

Customer name

[phone]

Phone

[product_type]

Product type

[product]

Product

[serial]

Serial

[disease]

Defect

[cond]

Condition

[complect]

Completeness

[cost]

Approx. cost

[acceptor]

Acceptor

Act of executed works and Warranty Card

Printed when gives order with repair.


Filenames — ./print_forms/act_works.xml, ./print_forms/warranty_works.xml

Available fields:


Tag:

Field in the screen form:

[number]

Order #

[date]

Iss. date

[customer]

Customer name (from Order)

[product_type]

Product type (from Order)

[product]

Product (from Order)

[serial]

Serial (from Order)

[giver]

Issued by

[warranty]

Warranty

[NN]

Number of row in the table (required tag)

[work]

Name of work (only in the table)

[quant]

Quantity of works (only in the table)

[summ]

Sum of the costs of work and parts (only in the table)



When printing cost of the each work is calculated as follows: Costs = ( SumOfWork + SumOfParts) / NumberOfWorks. Price calculation and the total amount should be made by QtRpt.
