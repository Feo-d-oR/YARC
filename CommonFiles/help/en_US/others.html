Print handwritten documents

In case of a power outage and site visits.


Printing on your default printer without preview. For orders are created blank entries in the database (in order to maintain a uniform numbering).
Value «reserved» in the "product" is used in the order editing handler to create a new customer record when you fill the order.

Order - order_hw.xml

Diagnosis act - act_diag_hw.xml

Act of executed works - act_works_hw.xml
